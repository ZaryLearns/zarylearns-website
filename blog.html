Renewable and Nonrenewable Energy

Renewable energy is one of the most promising and exciting developments of the 21st century. As the world continues to grapple with the consequences of climate change and fossil fuel depletion, renewable energy offers a way to power our societies sustainably and with less environmental impact. Renewable energy refers to energy that is generated from natural resources that are replenished over time, such as solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, and biomass. Unlike fossil fuels, which are finite resources, renewable energy sources have the potential to provide unlimited amounts of energy, without contributing to greenhouse gas emissions or other negative environmental impacts...
